The Roque Center

The Roque Center was founded in March of 1972 as a nonprofit corporation by a group of caring men and women whose lives had been touched, in one form or another, by alcoholism or drug addiction. Our mission and vision remains: “To help those who suffer from alcoholism and drug addiction and provide them access to affordable substance abuse treatment, while turning no one away for lack of ability to pay.”

Our 12-step based approach to recovery focuses on affecting positive change in the client’s lifestyle and behavior through education, group process, one-to-one counseling, exposure to alternative coping skills, and positive role models. With the realization that recovery is a process and not an event, our social model peer-oriented and home-like recovery program is designed to assist men and women in developing a foundation for long term recovery.

Since 1972 we have helped over 34,000 individuals receive the quality care and treatment they deserve.
